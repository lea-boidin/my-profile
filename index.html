I studied at the George Washington University for 1 year from 2018 to 2019. International Business was my major, I loved the GW Business School and made many new friends. GW Women in Business is a wonderful group of women on campus that I joined. We organized a conference with around 500 attendees on the theme of "Transform and Transcend". Learning how to communicate with company managers and potential sponsors for our event was enriching and enlightening.
